Investor Portal ~ Commonly Used Terms

Wallet Amount of cash available in your account.
Portfolio Value The current projected value of your portfolio, including your wallet value and exclusive of any prior defaults .
Principal Investment This is the amount of your principal investment in your portfolio.
Total Amount Invested This refers to the Total amount of money invested in your portfolio
Current Invested Current amount of principal you have invested in your portfolio.
Average Daily Balance The weighted average of capital you have since the inception of your account
Number of merchants Total number of merchants in which your portfolio invested
RTR RTR is Right To Return. It the total Returns your portfolio is projected to receive.
CTD CTD is Cash to Date. It represents the Cash collected to date in your portfolio. It represents the total amount of cash that is to be deposited. This can be calculated by subtracting the sum of Syndicate Fee and Management Fee from the total amount of money paid by a particular Participant.
Blended ROI Annualized ROI rate of your current portfolio.
Funded Amount This refers to the amount that was funded to a merchant.
Total Funded Amount This refers to the cost incurred by the company while funding a merchant.
Payment Amount This the gross amount of money to be paid back, with reference to the Funded amount.
Funded Date Make a profit, wherein Profit = profit – defaults
Factor Rate A factor rate is a flat fee that tells the total cost of a merchant cash advance. Usually, this is quoted as a decimal number (not a percentage) which acts as a multiplier. On an average, factor rates tend to start from around 1 going up to 2.
Default Amount The total amount of money that has been defaulted.
Default Rate Percent of total defaults against you principal investments.
Management Fee This is the fee paid to a person or company for managing a business, property, sum of money etc. on their behalf.
Over Payment This is the amount of money that is paid extra over the amount that is collected over RTR
ROI Make a profit, wherein Profit = profit – defaults
Origination Fee This can be defined as a fee charged by a lender on entering into a loan agreement to cover the cost of processing the loan. This is valid only when created the Investor is availed through CRM.
Up Sell Commission Make a profit, wherein Profit = profit – defaults
Flat Under Writing Fee It is a fixed amount of fee for every merchants. The amount at present is fixed at $350 and it is valid for merchants who are created through CRM only.
Under Writing Fee An underwriting fee for the service of evaluating the loan application for approval is a non-recurring fee that the lender may charge in lieu of an origination fee, or in addition to it.
