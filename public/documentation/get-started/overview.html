Investor Portal ~ Merchant Cash Advance Business Given a New Direction

We are proud to present you “The Investor Portal”, our proprietary software that helps manage all the different aspects of servicing and syndication of the Merchant Cash Advance Industry. We have given a new direction to the Investment Business. As an Investment Business Advisor, you face a daily challenge to keep a track of all your financial assets and to deliver outstanding advice and service while efficiently managing your Investment business. The Investment portal streamlines the financial management process of your business bringing unparalleled flexibility, accuracy, performance and value. Our design gives access to multiple companies, investors, merchants giving Admin permission for overall supervision. It helps give you options to integrate a few or many applications and capabilities according to the particular needs of your business. We present you an overview of all features available to you at your service.

Integrated portfolio and practice management

Through the Investor Portal, you can leverage wealth management tools to address your front-, middle-, and back-office needs. Our broad range of innovative technology--analytics, advisory tools, advanced portfolio solutions, and practice management and reporting capabilities--spans everything you need to help deliver better outcomes to your clients.

Our Investor Portal features include:

  • Creation of Admin, Companies, Lenders, Merchants, Investors
  • The Admin enjoys the permission to create and edit all involved parties
  • Company will be able to only create Investors and manage the funds given to the Merchants under him
  • The Admin can overview the total flow of wealth in his network, thus allowing proper and planned wealth management
  • Various reports, that can be queried based on different criteria, assist in the overall functions of the admin
  • And much more ……
