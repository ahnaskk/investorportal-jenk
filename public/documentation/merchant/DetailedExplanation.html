Welcome to Merchant Portal!!

Merchants are the borrowers in the MCA industry. Being a merchant, you can submit your requests to the Lenders who in turn forward them to the Companies. Once request are passed, they are released to you through the Lenders.

What you can do through Merchant Portal?
  • Through this Portal, you can view the current status of you monetary assistance.
  • View the number of Payments left
  • Request to Payoff or to avail more money can be done very easily
  • View your transactions till date, arranged date-wise, with the Payment amount and Payments completed
  • You have also been given a privilege to view statements of your transactions

Overview of the Merchant Portal

Login to view Portfolio

The very first step to use the Velocity Merchant Portal, you need to log on to the application using your registered email ID and password. The login page looks like the one displayed below:

    • Step 1 - Login Page
      • Enter your registered email ID in the space for email
      • Enter your registered Password id in the space for password
      • Click the Login button
      • If all the entered details are correct, then you will be redirected to you Portfolio Dashboard
    • Step 2 - Forgot Password
      • In case you are not able to recollect your password for some reason, you can click on the Forgot Password link
      • In the given space, please enter your registered email ID and click on the button "Send Password Reset Link"
      • You will receive a link as an email to your registered email ID for resetting your password
Portfolio Dashboard

Once you have logged in successfully, you are automatically taken to your Portfolio dashboard as given below:

    • Step 1 - Side Navigation Bar
      • Get familiar with the side navigation menu from the figure given below:
      • You can use the these navigation links to perform different functions
    • Step 2 - Navigate through the portfolio
      • The menu items are as given below:
        • Dashboard
        • Transactions
        • Make Payment
        • FAQ
        • User
    • Step 3 - Dashboard
      • Various values are displayed on the dashboard
      • The values are as follows:
        • Funded Amount - This refers to the amount that has been funded to a merchant
        • Factor Rate - A factor rate is a flat fee that tells the total cost of a merchant cash advance. Usually, this is quoted as a decimal number (not a percentage) which acts as a multiplier. On an average, factor rates tend to start from around 1 going up to 2
        • Total Payments - Refers to the allotted number of payment schedules
        • Actual Payments Left Refers to the number of payment schedules remaining to payoff the RTR
        • Payment Amount - This the gross amount of money to be paid back at each instance, with reference to the Funded amount
        • RTR - RTR is Right To Return. It the total returns your portfolio is projected to return
        • Balance - This is the balance amount that needs to be paid. It is calculated by deducting the payment amount from the RTR

      • An option to Request Payoff your remaining amount has been provided here.
      • Sometimes you might want some more amount of financial assistance. For such situations, another option has been provided to Request More Money to request for the same.
Transactions

All transactions as a part of payback are recorded and displayed here as shown below:

      • Data is segregated under three categories:
        • Payment Date - The date on which payment was made by the user
        • Payment Amount - The amount of money that was paid back on that particular date
        • Payment Balance - The balance amount that is remaining to be paid back
Make Payment

Payment can be made using credit card.

    • Step 1 - Know the amount
      • The amount to be paid is displayed here
      • This includes a processing fee as mentioned
    • Step 2 - Make the required Payment
      • Enter the required details :
      • Amount, Name on Card, Payment Type, Amount to be paid, Card Number, Expiry Date and CVV
      • Click on the Submit Button to make the payment
Two Step Verification

You can enable a two-factor authentication. This enables an extra layer of security.

    • Step 1 - Enable Two Factor Authentication
      • The steps to be followed to enable two step authentication has been provided here
    • Step 2 - Download and Install an Authenticator App in your mobile
    • Step 3 - Scan the QR Code shown in the given page
    • Step 4 - Once the QR Code is scanned, a 6-digit code will be given. You need to enter the codein the space provided.
    • Step 5 - Click on the Connect Phone button. This will enable two-factor authentication. From then on, each time you will be prompted a secure token from your authenticator app installed in your mobile
Request Payoff
  • To request payoff from the dashboard:
    • Step 1 - Click on the on the Request Payoff button on the dashboard screen
      • On clicking, a request is sent to the admin
    • Step 2 - Admin will verify and confirm the request
Request More Money
  • To request for more money from the dashboard:
    • Step 1 - Click on the on the Request More Money button on the dashboard screen
      • A window as shown below pops up
      • Enter the amount of more money you are requesting for
      • Click Confirm to send a request
      • Click Cancel if you do not want to request for the time being
    • Step 2 - Admin will verify and confirm the request
