Step 1
Login to your account

  • To Log in, you need to enter your registered email ID and your password and press enter or click on the log in button.
  • In case, you have forgotten your password, you need to click on the 'Forgot Password' link. This will take you to a page as shown below:
  • You have to enter your registered Email-id, wherein you will receive a link to reset your password and enter a new one.
  • Once you have done resetting your password, log in with your registered Email id and your new password.
