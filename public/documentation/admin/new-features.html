Merchants - Assign based on Liquidity

A new feature, Investor Assignment based on Liquidity has been added to the portal. The details and the logic of the feature is as given below:

Assign based on Liquidity
  • This feature is available to merchants who have not been invested. We can filter such merchants by clicking on the "Not Invested" checkbox.
  • All the merchants that have Not Invested is tabulated and displayed. On viewing these individual merchants, we can get their details in the respective portfolio, as shown below
  • The merchant needs to be in the Active status to avail this feature.
  • A button "Assign Investors" is present, which on clicking helps to assign investors. A pop-up appears wherein you can Select Company. By default, all investors will be selected. Options are provided to either Unselect or Select All Investors and then Assign them.
  • The Investors are assigned with a new amount depending on their Liquidity.

Logic Used in "Assign based on Liquidity" feature
  • For merchants who are currently Not Invested or were in the Cancelled Status can be assigned
  • The status can be changed to Active and then proceed with the Assignment
  • The Funded Amount of Investors are changed. The Funded Amount of the Investors who have invested the least are rounded off to the nearest lower dollar amount. These individual penny amount are added up and are distributed to highest invested Investor/s and their Funded Amount is Increased.
  • Accordingly, the RTR, Invested Amount and the Liquidity of the Investors will also change depending on the changed Funded Amount.
