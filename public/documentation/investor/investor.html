Welcome to Investor Portal!!

Merchant cash advances provide short-term lump sums of working capital to a business for a specific and immediate need. In return, a business pays a percentage of their future sales for the security of an instant cash injection.

Being a Investor in the MCA Business, an overall overview of your financial status, your RTRs and CTDs, proves advantageous for your business.

What you can do through Investor Portal?
  • Through this Portal, you can view the current status of your investment.
  • Detailed view of all the Merchants you are financing and their current status.
  • Generate Reports and Statements according to your need.
Overview of the Investor Portal

Ability to be in track of your portfolio is the main feature of the Investor Portal.The first screen is that of the dashboard, where in you get your main menu.

The about screenshot gives you the main Menu items available to you. You have been given all the options that can help to keep track of your finances.

Dashboard

The image below shows the Dashboard. The Financial Assistance provided to different merchants are shown here:

  • Dashboard presents the overall status of your financial interactions.
  • An overview of your wallet status is presented here. An option to add money is also provided here, which when clicked, a pop-up, as given below, will help you add money.
  • Pending ACH Credit and Debit amount displayed.
  • Principal Investment, Projected Portfolio Value and number of merchants displayed. This allows you to understand your financial investment status at one go.
  • Blended ROI, ROI, Default Rate, Paid To Date values displayed as a separate highlight. This allows you to understand your financial investment status at one go.
  • View all button in the Last Payment highlight box takes us to the Payment report.
  • Graphical representation of the financial status is included here. Monthly points have been plotted when, when mouse-overed, displays the value for RTR, CTD and Funded Amount.
  • Generated Statements also included here.

Bank
  • Banking Feature displays the current Bank Account details.
  • Option to add money and withdraw money has been provided so as to enable you to manage your finances.
  • Stripe Payment has been integrated for the ease of payment.
  • Option to add another bank account is also provided
  • The option to Edit and Delete has also been given here. If you want to edit, you are taken to a pop-up as shown below, wherein you can update the details.
  • Option to select how often we would like to receive the Payouts.
