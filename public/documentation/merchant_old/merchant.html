Welcome to Merchant Portal!!

Merchants are the borrowers in the MCA industry. Being a merchant, you can submit your requests to the Lenders who in turn forward them to the Companies. Once request are passed, they are released to you through the Lenders.

What you can do through Merchant Portal?
  • Through this Portal, you can view the current status of you monetary assistance.
  • View the number of Payments left.
  • Request to Payoff or to avail more money can be done very easily.
  • View your transactions till date, arranged date-wise, with the Payment amount and Payments completed.
  • You have also been given a privilege to view statements of your transactions.
Overview of the Merchant Portal

Being a Merchant, it is always helpful to know your current status. Ability to be in track of your portfolio is the main feature of the Merchant's portal.The first screen is that of the dashboard, where in you get your main menu.
