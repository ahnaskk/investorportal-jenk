Welcome To The Merchant Portal!!

A merchant cash advance (MCA) is a cash advance based upon the credit card sales deposited in a business’ merchant account. A business owner(merchant) can apply for an MCA and have funds deposited into a business checking account fairly quickly—sometimes as quickly as 24 hours after approval.
